Our Story

SpotOnTrip started with a simple idea: travel planning shouldn't be a headache. We realized that people spend more time organizing spreadsheets than actually enjoying the anticipation of their trip.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to bring all your travel needs—hotels, flights, and hidden gems—into one simple, easy-to-use platform. Whether you are a solo backpacker or planning a family vacation, we are here to make it spot on.
